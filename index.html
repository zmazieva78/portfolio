About Me

I was born there, finished school and university and moved to the United States back in 2004. I hold Master of Science degree in Economics from Bishkek Humanities University. My diploma thesis was about “Advantages and disadvantages of Free Economic Zones and their impact on National Economy”. Besides English I can also speak Russian.

After moving to the United States, I mostly worked in banking industry and trading stocks as a hobby. My first position was with US Bank as an banker. My last position was with Bank of America as a Relationship Manager. During my career I learned how to help customers by understanding their financial situation and offering them the right solution and product.

Now I am taking actions toward my new carrier and studying Web Development at Epicodus. During this course I will able to implement a few projects using JavaScript, React.js, C#, Bootstrap, HTML, CSS etc.

I'm aspiring to become a user-focused Web Developer with a passion for making technologies to solve real life problems and improve user experience. I enjoy writing algorithms and solving complicated technical problems with unique perspectives on how end-users interact with websites and software platforms. I'm focused on delivering working applications according to stakeholder's expectations and users' feedback.
